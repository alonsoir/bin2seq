
 Download platform pre-built distribution from http://www.hdfgroup.org/products/java/release/download.html
 and add it to JVM 
 e.g. -Dncsa.hdf.hdf5lib.H5.hdf5lib=C:\Projects\Java\hdf-java\native\hdf5lib\jhdf5.dll
 otherwise, will get this problem:
 java.lang.UnsatisfiedLinkError: no jhdf5 in java.library.path
